# FriendZones Website Design Direction

## Subject
A hospitality and dining group in Phan Thiết – Mũi Né. The homepage helps travelers and local customers quickly choose a place to stay, eat, or meet.

## Visual direction
“Coastal daylight into nightlife”: deep ocean green, sea-glass teal, sun yellow and warm coral. The page moves from a dark cinematic hero into bright daytime content, then back to an evening feature.

## Signature
The hero uses three connected photographic “moments” — morning by the sea, afternoon around a table, and night by the pool — linked by a dotted route. It makes the breadth of FriendZones understandable without explaining company structure.
